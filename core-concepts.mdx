## Risk Management Principles

You protect capital first. Position size never exceeds a fixed percentage of your account, and every trade includes a predefined stop-loss.

```python
account_balance = 50000
risk_percent = 0.01
stop_distance = 25  # pips

position_size = (account_balance * risk_percent) / stop_distance
print(f"Trade size: {position_size:.2f} lots")
```
