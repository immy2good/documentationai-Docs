## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
Before you begin, ensure you have a funded trading account on MT4 or MT5 and access to the members area. You will also need the latest version of the platform installed on your computer.
</Callout>

## Account Setup and Login

Follow these steps to create your account and access the tools.

<Steps>
<Step title="Create Your Account" icon="user-plus">
Visit the official registration page and complete the signup form with your email and basic details. You will receive a confirmation link within minutes.
</Step>
<Step title="Verify and Login" icon="log-in">
Check your email for the verification message, then head to the members portal at `https://my.itradeaims.net/`. Enter your credentials to access the dashboard.
</Step>
<Step title="Download Resources" icon="download">
Once logged in, navigate to the downloads section and select the package matching your platform (MT4 or MT5).
</Step>
</Steps>

## Installing MT4/MT5 Tools

Install the indicators and expert advisors using the platform's built-in file manager.

<Tabs>
<Tab title="MT4" icon="monitor">
````bash
# Copy the downloaded .ex4 and .dll files
# Navigate to File > Open Data Folder in MT4
# Paste files into MQL4/Indicators and MQL4/Experts folders
# Restart the platform to load the tools
````
</Tab>
<Tab title="MT5" icon="monitor">
````bash
# Copy the downloaded .ex5 and .dll files
# Navigate to File > Open Data Folder in MT5
# Paste files into MQL5/Indicators and MQL5/Experts folders
# Restart the platform to load the tools
````
</Tab>
</Tabs>

## First Trade Walkthrough

<Steps>
<Step title="Attach the Indicator" icon="bar-chart">
Open a chart for your preferred market (Forex, Indices, or Crypto). Drag the Banana Indicator or AIMS Algo from the Navigator panel onto the chart.
</Step>
<Step title="Apply Basic Settings" icon="settings">
Right-click the indicator and set the timeframe to match your strategy. Enable alerts for entry signals.
</Step>
<Step title="Execute the Trade" icon="play">
When a clear signal appears, place your order with the recommended lot size and stop-loss levels shown on the chart.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Basic Configuration Steps

Configure your risk parameters in the tool settings panel. Use the following example configuration for a conservative approach:

```json
{
  "riskPerTrade": 1.0,
  "maxDailyLoss": 3.0,
  "alertSound": true,
  "autoLotSize": false
}
```

<Callout kind="tip">
Always test your configuration in a demo account first. Adjust the `riskPerTrade` value based on your account size and risk tolerance.
</Callout>
