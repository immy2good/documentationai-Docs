---
title: Cannot Access Dashboard
description: Quick guide for access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Login page keeps refreshing
- Access denied error on dashboard

## Causes

- Incorrect login credentials
- Account not verified
- Browser cache problems

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check credentials">
    Try resetting your password if unsure.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify account">
    Confirm your email and activate your account from the link sent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Log out, clear cache, and try again.
  </Step>
</Steps>
