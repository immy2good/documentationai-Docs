## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to platform">
    Visit `https://imranyousafzai.com` and sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Projects">
    Click the "Projects" tab in the left sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create new project">
    Click "New Project", enter name and description.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your project">
    Click "Save" to finish. Start adding docs from the project dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
